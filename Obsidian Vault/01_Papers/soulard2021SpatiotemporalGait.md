## Study Context
Reliability of a foot mounted [[IMU]]: Physiolog5, 200 Hz. 
Didn't have a reference system
Compared their ability to capture spatiotemporal metrics within and between conditions
Near perfect ICC in average metrics
Pretty detailed paper with good equation definitions

Population:
- [[Healthy Adults]]

Sample Size:
- 10 Men, 10 Women
- Three trials per condition

Activity:
- [[Normal Walking]]: 10 metres
- [[Dual Task]]: carry a full cup of water in their dominant hand with the  following instruction: “perform both tasks as well as possible"

Methods:
- [[IMU]]

Statistics:
- [[Repeated Measures ANOVA]]
- [[ICC]] 2,1 absolute
- [[MDC]]
- [[SEM]]
