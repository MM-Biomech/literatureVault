## Study Context

Population:
- [[Healthy Adults]] (N=20, age 44.9 ± 11.7 yr, 10M/10F)

Activity:
- [[Straight-line walking]] (10MWT, 14m walkway, comfortable speed)
- [[Dual-task Walking]] (carry full cup of water)

Methods:
- [[IMU]] (Physilog5, foot-worn, 200 Hz)

Statistics:
- [[ICC]] (2,1 absolute agreement)
- [[SEM]]
- [[MDC]]
- [[Repeated Measures ANOVA]]

Sample Size:
- 20 (10M, 10F), 3 trials per condition

## Key Insights Extracted

- [[ICC and MDC are useful reliability metrics when no reference system is available]]
- [[Symmetry Index has low intra-session reliability in healthy adults with short walking trials]]
