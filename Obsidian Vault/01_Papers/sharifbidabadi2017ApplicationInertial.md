## Study Context
%% begin study-context %%
Population:
- [[Healthy Adults]] 
- [[Foot Drop]]

Sample Size:
 - 2: one healthy, one FD
Activity:
- [[Straight-line walking]]

Methods:
- [[IMU]] They made their own custom. Placed on the thigh, shank, and foot

Statistics:
- None. Observational

Purpose / Hypothesis:
To introduce an IMU-based system which can be used in gait analysis to capture movements of each leg section, in particular, for foot drop condition


%% end study-context %%
