# [[Stroop Word Test]]

Type: Clinical Measure

Definition:
1) read the name of colors (W)
2) identify the color of rectangles patches (C)
3) identify the color of the ink of words with incongruent color-word combinations (CW). 
Recite as many responses as possible over thirty seconds. 

Equation:
The interference score is calculated as
$$
\text{IG} = CW -\frac{\text{(W * C)}}{\text{W + C}}
$$
IG: interference score. CW, W, and C are the number of correct responses in the CW, W, and C conditions,  respectively.
Larger IG values reflect better scores.
