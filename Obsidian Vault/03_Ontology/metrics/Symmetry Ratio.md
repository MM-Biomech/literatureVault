# Symmetry Ratio (SR)

Type: Metric

Definition:
The ratio of a spatiotemporal parameter between the right and left limb. Easier to interpret clinically than [[Symmetry Index]].

Equation:
$$
SR = \frac{X_R}{X_L}
$$

Domain: [[Asymmetry]]

Unit: dimensionless (1.0 = perfect symmetry; 2.0 = right is twice the value of left)
